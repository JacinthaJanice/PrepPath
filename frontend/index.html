Aptitude Strategy
For Sierra ODC & Campus Placements
SMART PREP
Quantitative (Tue–Thu)
Master These 6 First
Percentages, Profit/Loss, Time-Speed-Distance, Time & Work, SI/CI, Ratios. These 6 cover 70% of all placement tests. Do them in this exact order.
Logical (Friday only)
Pattern Recognition Daily
Syllogisms, Blood Relations, Seating Arrangements, Coding-Decoding. One deep session Friday beats scattered practice daily. Draw diagrams always.
Verbal Ability
Read 1 Article Daily
The Hindu or BBC. 15 min. Para-completion, RC and vocabulary improve naturally. Use it as morning warm-up before music.
Speed & Accuracy
Always Use a Timer
Aim 1.5 min/question for quant, 1 min for logical. Full mocks on weekends: PrepInsta, AmbitionBox, IndiaBix. Review every wrong answer.
